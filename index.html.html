Our Story

Rooted in Vermont,
raised with care

Open since 2010, Farmhouse Tap & Grill has been a cornerstone of Burlington's farm-to-table movement. We build our menu around the freshest available ingredients — showcasing the best of what is grown, raised, and produced right here in Vermont.

From LaPlatte River Angus beef to Does' Leap feta, from Champlain Valley Creamery cheese to Red Hen bread — every dish tells a local story. Our Tap Room pours highly prized craft beers from Vermont's backyard and beyond, and our seasonal Beer Garden is a Burlington institution.

"The tap room delivers highly prized beers from Vermont's backyard and beyond."

The Experience

More than a meal —
a Vermont tradition

I
The Tap Room
Our main dining room and bar, open for lunch and dinner daily. Online reservations available. Spacious waiting areas with full bar service if there's a wait.
II
The Parlor Bar
Our downstairs Parlor bar offers the full menu in a more intimate setting. Perfect for date nights, small groups, or a quiet drink after work.
III
Beer Garden
Our seasonal outdoor Beer Garden is open first come, first served. A beloved Burlington gathering spot — bring friends, enjoy Vermont's finest pours under open skies.
IV
Weekend Brunch
Saturday and Sunday brunch served from 11am. Full brunch menu available in the dining room, Parlor bar, and Beer Garden. No reservations needed for outdoor seating.
V
Private Dining
Host your next event at Farmhouse. We offer fully customizable food and cocktail menus for private events. Contact us to discuss your needs.
VI
Taco Tuesdays
Weekly Taco Tuesdays hosted in the Beer Garden of our sister restaurant El Cortijo. A Burlington tradition — join the neighborhood every week.
